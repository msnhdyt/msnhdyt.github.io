What is Onet Prime?

Onet Prime is a game to find sequence number that the sum is prime number.

How to play?

Find the sequence by clicking two boxes. If the sum of the sequence is prime then you will get a score. If it's not, your score will be decreased. If the score doesn't change it means you have already chosen the boxes.
